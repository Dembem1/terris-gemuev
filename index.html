Terris Gemuev Architects is a UK based international architecture and design practice, with studios in Glasgow, Munich and Moscow.
We specialise in high-performance, energy-efficient design, delivering residential, industrial and mixed-use projects for clients across Europe and Asia. With more than 35 years of experience, our work combines technical rigour, creative thinking and a collaborative approach to produce sustainable, future-focused solutions.
